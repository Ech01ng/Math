Cofactor of a Matrices

Formula:
C<sub>i j = [(-1)<sup>i + j] [|D<sub>i j|]

Where:
C = Cofactor
i = Row
j = Column
D = Determinant
sub = subscript notation

Example:
Given ->

A=
|1 -1 -1|
|2 3 8 |
|-3 2 1 |

Find C<sub>1 1

Process:

D<sub>1 1=
|3 8 |
|2 1 |

C<sub>1 1 = [(-1)<sup>1 + 1] [|D<sub>i j|]

C<sub 1 1> = (1)(3x1 - 8x2)
C<sub>1 1 = 3 - 16
C<sub>1 1 = -13
