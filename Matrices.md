## Determinant of a Matrix

<H2>For a 2x2 Matrix</H2>
Given:<br>
|a b|<br>
|c d|

<H3>Process:</H3>

ad - bc

<H2>For a 3x3 Matrix</H2>

|1 -1 -1|<br>
|2 3 8 |<br>
|-3 2 1 |

<H3>Process:</H3>

Similar to the 2x2 but with an extra step

|1 -1 -1|<br>
|2 3 8 |<br>
|-3 2 1 |

| D<sub>i j</sub> |

## Cofactor of a Matrix

<H2>Formula:</H2>
C<sub>i j</sub> = (-1)<sup>i + j</sup> x | D<sub>i j</sub> |

<H3>Where:</H3>
C = Cofactor<br>
i = Row<br>
j = Column<br>
D = Determinant

<H2>Example:</H2>
Given ->

A=<br>
|1 -1 -1|<br>
|2 3 8 |<br>
|-3 2 1 |

Find C<sub>1 1</sub>

<H2>Process:</H2>

D<sub>1 1</sub>=<br>
|3 8 |<br>
|2 1 |

C<sub>1 1</sub> = (-1)<sup>1 + 1</sup> x | D<sub>i j</sub> |

C<sub>1 1</sub> = (1) (3x1 - 8x2)

C<sub>1 1</sub> = 3 - 16

C<sub>1 1</sub> = -13
